Pizza Bagels

Description

Easy pizza bagels

Ingredients

  • Bagels. I use Everything Bagels
  • *Pizza Sauce
  • Favorite shredded cheese
  • Your favorite pizza toppings.
  • **Cream Cheese

*Spaghetti sauce can be substituted for Pizza sauce

**If you have never tried cream cheese on pizza, Do it!

Steps

  1. **Spread a layer of cream cheese on the bagel.
  2. Spread Sauce on bagel.
  3. Put desired amount of cheese on bagel
  4. Place desired toppings
  5. Cook in oven at 350 for 30 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
