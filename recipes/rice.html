Ryoh's Easy White Rice

Description

A simple stovetop recipe that ensures your rice is fully cooked.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Water
  • Salt*

*Salt is optional

Steps

  1. Add however much rice you want to cook to the pan.
  2. Wash rice throughly. At least three rinses should do.
  3. Fill the pan with water until it covers the rice by about an inch. Add salt if you choose here.
  4. Heat rice until boiling
  5. Reduce heat to low and cover
  6. Cook for 15 minutes
  7. Remove pan from heat Don't take off lid
  8. Let rest for additional 15 minutes
  9. Enjoy
